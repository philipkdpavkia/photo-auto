Frequently Asked Questions

What camera gear should I bring?

Bring your camera body and a versatile range of lenses. A standard zoom (24-70mm) and a portrait lens (85mm or similar) are recommended. Don't forget extra batteries, memory cards, and a laptop for editing sessions.

Is this workshop suitable for beginners?

Yes! The workshop is designed for photographers of all skill levels. We'll provide personalized guidance based on your experience.

What's included in the price?

The price includes accommodation, local transportation, workshop sessions, and photography guidance. Flights are not included.

How do I reserve my spot?

A 30% deposit is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance is due 60 days before the workshop starts.

What's the accommodation like?

We stay in carefully selected local guesthouses and hotels that offer comfort while maintaining an authentic connection to local culture.

Do I need a visa for Senegal, Mexico and Cuba?

Visa requirements vary by your nationality. We'll provide detailed information about visa requirements after booking.

What about meals?

Meals are at your own expense, giving you the flexibility to explore local cuisine.
