Dia de Muertos

Document one of Mexico's most significant cultural celebrations in the mountains of Oaxaca. Traditional ceremonies, street photography, and authentic encounters.

DURING THE WORKSHOP YOU WILL LEARN:

  • Travel photography approach techniques
  • How to build a visual narrative
  • Natural light management
  • Daily editing of your images

We will stay in selected accommodations that will allow us to experience local culture up close. The group will be limited to 8 participants to ensure an optimal learning experience.
