No Theory, Just Practice

Forget endless theoretical lessons. Here we go out, we walk, we chase the light, we wait for the right moment. Photography is about practice, patience, and movement. No need to talk about it for hours: we need to do it.

My workshops are designed for those who want to get their hands dirty with real photography. No frills, no complicated speeches. Just you, your camera, and unique locations where you can challenge yourself.

I like working with small, motivated groups, with people willing to wake up at dawn, walk all day, return to the same spot multiple times until the shot is right. Photography is physical work, as well as mental.

How We Work

  • Only field practice, from dawn to dusk
  • Small groups for maximum attention
  • Daily photo reviews
  • Total immersion in location
